About LifeBeyondSugar

LifeBeyondSugar is a website dedicated to educating people about the negative impact of sugar and the benefits of reducing sugar intake. Our mission is to help people lead healthier and happier lives by making better dietary choices.

. Did you know that the average consumption is 17 teaspoons of added sugar per day? . Did you know that sugary drinks are the largest source of added sugar in diet? . Did you know that excessive sugar consumption has been linked to an increased risk of developing heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and certain cancers? . Did you know that the World Health Organization recommends that adults limit their daily sugar intake to less than 6 teaspoons?
